Biography

Resident DJ for Shanghai-based collective Antigen, Haomiiya navigates the electronic scenes between China and South Korea. With over 16 years of drumming experience, her music is percussion-led and syncopated — blending footwork, Detroit techno, Miami bass and hardgroove.

Her work has included performances with Pearson Sound, Kode9, TDJ, Jesse Kanda & Doon Kanda, Teklife (Traxman, DJ Spinn), Foodman, DJ Plead and Helena Hauff. She has also featured at brand events for JW Anderson, Salomon, Hypebeast and Soulgoods.
